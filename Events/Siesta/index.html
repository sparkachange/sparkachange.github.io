Siesta! The football fiesta!

¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Arguably the world's most popular sport, the game of football is simply incredible. The adrenaline of the game, the strategies we put into action and the camaraderie make every game an incredible experience. Needless to say, the Spark A Change team has been bewitched by the sorceress that is football, and thus held a football tournament to celebrate our love for the sport, and to raise funds for incredible organizations.

This has been one of our flagship events since our conception. Till date, we have conducted 3 tournaments over 4 years in multiple venues hosting over 70 teams and over 500 players with players as young as 12 to as old as 23. Siesta presently has been conducted in a variety prestigious football field in Bangalore and has partnered with several organisations including but not limited to Rebel Foundation, Shraddha Eye Care Trust, Samarthanam Trust.

Siesta has been able to raise over ₹30,000 for our partners and have gotten excellent feedback from both our partners and all our participants over the years. With many participants coming back to us every year for the tournament
