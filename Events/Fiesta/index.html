Fiesta

¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Fiesta, A fundraiser conducted in honour of Republic Day on the 26th of January was a major sports athletics meet held at Akme Harmony. The event included various sports such as a Football, Basketball, Badminton, Table Tennis as well as various Athletic events for kids and adults of all age groups. The main objective was to host a carnival and integrating it with sports in order to raise funds for the underprivileged. The event was conducted successfully which helped us raise funds of more than 25 Thousand Rupees which were donated to Samarthanam Trust for the upliftment and education of primary school children. The event was a huge success with members throughout the apartment helping in order to bring it together for the common welfare of society.
