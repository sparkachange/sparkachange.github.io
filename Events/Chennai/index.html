Help Chennai Today

Relief Effort

¯\_(ツ)_/¯

The lives of thousands were impacted by the Chennai floods. In order to do our part and improve the lives of those affected we decided to hold collection drives throughout Bangalore and raise awareness. We wished to make a large impact on our society by not only receiving funds but also bringing people together and work together to help those who were in need. The members stood till late in the night on the road giving out posters and collecting the smallest of donations. Spark A Change successfully implemented the project by collecting huge amounts of donations and is one of our most successful projects. Several organizations came together in order to support our initiative. Seeing the unity between all the people of our community coming together to take part in our initiative truly provided us with a sense of gratitude and satisfaction and restored our faith in humanity. We truly enjoyed conducting this project and will continue to impact those around us for the better.
