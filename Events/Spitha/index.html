Spitha

Where your talent makes a difference

¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Our maiden event, a fundraising talent show where people of all skill levels came together to show off their talents! The show was the perfect platform for participants of all ages to present their art in front of a vivacious audience.

The show ran for over 5 hours with a huge variety of performances and participants from multiple singers to dancers and acrobats to heavy metal bands. This event was an enormous success with around 25 different performances and an audience of 200 members. The event was able to raise close to ₹1,00,000. The money we raised was donated to the Samarthanam trust, a school for the blind.

Spitha was a culmination of talent, showmanship and the joy of giving and its success confirmed our team's commitment towards helping make the world a better place.
