What we do

Fundraisers

Using our incredible team's varied interests as our driving force, we host events of a plethora of themes through which we raise funds for NGOs.

Collection Drives


Where there is a need, there is a drive! Spark A Change collects and transports items of various types for NGOs.

Relief

In the dire event of a natural disaster, we raise funds and collect care packages to send with volunteers to help mitigate the pain of the affected areas.

Our Previous Projects


Spítha I

Our maiden event, a fundraising talent show where people of all skill levels came together to show off their talents! The show was the perfect platform for children of all ages to present their art in front of a vivacious audience. The money we raised was donated to the Samarthanam trust, a school for the blind. Spitha was a culmination of talent, showmanship and the joy of giving and its success confirmed our teams commitment towards helping make the world a better place.
