Help Chennai Today

The end of 2015 brought a terrible storm to the shores of Chennai and surrounding areas in the state of Tamil Nadu. Spark A Change, in collaboration with concerned citizens of the city of Bangalore, raised funds and collected inventory of care packages. Using the funds raised, we purchased additional care packages containing essentials for those effected by the catastrophic flooding. Trucks filled with these packages were sent with volunteers who hand delivered them to the affected individuals.

Spítha!

Spark A Change was born to address a major problem faced my NGOs across the globe: funding. A team of enthusiastic students set course to bringing an end to this problem, and from their vision, Spítha was born. While discussing ways to raise funds, everyone came to a consensus that talent would be the center piece of our business plan, thus the idea of a fundraising talent show 'sparked' into our minds. Spítha, the Greek word for 'spark', was our idea of the perfect platform to provide children of all ages a stage and audience to showcase their talents to, no matter what their skill level. Additionally, for the frustrated office goer, Spítha is the perfect weekend event: a stress-free talent show that will leave them thoroughly entertained.

Thus, with the idea in place, we set about to make our vision a reality. Thanks to our sponsors, the AECS trust and Brigade Hospitality group, the talent show managed to raise an incredible amount of funds for the children of the Samarthanam trust! And not to mention, left a crowd with cherishable memories, and gave the performers a taste of the incredible rush of being under the spotlight.

Siesta!

From the kickoff to the final whistle, Siesta was truly an incredible tournament. Teams had come from far and wide to compete for a noble cause. The event supported the Shraddha Eye Care Trust, an organization that provides free cataract surgeries to underprivileged patients from rural areas.

Project: Dream

In our lives, we always draw inspiration from our childhood. The greatest joys of our childhood often come from playing with our beloved toys. What about those who aren't fortunate enough to have toys of their own? In an effort to bring smiles to the children of the Aahan Foundation, we held a toy collection drive. The toys were given to the children as presents for Diwali 2015.
