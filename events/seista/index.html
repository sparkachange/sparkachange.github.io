Siesta! The football fiesta!

Arguably the world's most popular sport, the game of football is simply incredible. The adrenaline of the game, the strategies we put into action and the comradarie make every game an incredible experience. Needless to say, the Spark A Change team has been bewitched by the sorceress that is football, and thus held a football tournament to celebrate our love for the sport, and to raise funds for an incredible organization.

The Shraddha Eye Care Trust is an organization based in Padmanambhanagara, Bangalore. This NGO conducts medical camps in rural areas in karnataka, transports villagers who are in need of cataract surgeries to their hospital, and provides free treatment. Their initiative is incredible, and Siesta's goal was to raise funds for this organization. Held in two categories, U-21 and U-15, the tournament attracted teams from far and wide. They competed fiercely, and every game was incredible to watch. Thanks to their passion for the sport, the event was a great success!

The champions!

The winners of the U21 tournament were awarded a trophy and a cash prize.

The U-15 Champions!

The valiant U-15 champions too were given a cash prize and a trophy to commemorate their efforts.
